Shinzō Abe

Shinzō Abe was born in Tokyo, Japan in 1954. In 1993 he got his first taste of politics when he was elected into the house of representatives. He later worked for his father, Abe Shintaro, as a secretary. He then was elected as Prime Minister from 2006 and was voted out in 2007. He then was voted back in 2012 and remains Prime Minister to this day.
