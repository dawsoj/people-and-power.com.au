Kim Jong un

Born 8th of January, 1984, Kim Jong-un was the son of North Korea’s supreme leader at the time, Kim Jong-il. In 2011, he took his father’s place as leader of North Korea. He was then reported to have ordered the execution of several political opponents. Kim Jong-il then authorised the development of nuclear and ballistic weapons. Donald Trump, who came to power a few years after Kim Jong, frequently debated about the testing of these weapons. To this day Kim Jong-Il still runs North Korea
