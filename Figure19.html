Jair Bolsonaro

Jair Bolsonaro is the president of Brazil, Jair was born on March 21st 1955. He currently is the 38th president of Brazil. Before he became president he was a military officer he soon became recognized when he wrote an article saying that military officers have low wages. He was president since January 1st 2019.
